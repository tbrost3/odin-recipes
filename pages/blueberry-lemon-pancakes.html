Blueberry Lemon Pancakes

A sweet & fresh spin on the breakfast classic!

Make a brunch-worthy breakfast entree quickly that won't break the bank.

  • 2 cups of Bisquick mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 16 oz fresh blueberries
  • Half a lemon's juice
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  1. Mix the Bisquick, milk, vegetable oil, and eggs together until the mixture is smooth without lumps
  2. In a medium saucepan, add the blueberries, lemon juice, and sugar over medium heat. Occasionally stir until the blueberries have begun to burst and the jam mixture is warm
  3. With a laddle, pour a scoop of the pancake batter onto a skillet preheated on medium-heat. Cook on one side until the edges dull and bubbles begin to pop in the batter. Flip over and cook until golden brown.
