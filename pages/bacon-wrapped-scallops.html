Bacon Wrapped Scallops

Pan-seared bacon wrapped scallops in less than 30 minutes!

A "Surf and Turf" recipe that everyone loves. Great for an appetizer to an event, or a date-night in with your partner.

  • 8 large sea scallops (1 pound)
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 thick-cut bacon slies (about 8 ounces
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degF. Set a rack on a rimmed baking sheet
  2. Season the scallops with salt and pepper and wrap each in 1 slice of bacon, securing it with a toothpick.
  3. Transfer to the prepared baking sheet and drizzle with the butter.
  4. Roast for 18 to 20 minutes, until the bacon and scallops are cooked through. Serve warm.
