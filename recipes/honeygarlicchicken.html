Honey-Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Description

I have used it often. It's easy and uses pantry staples. Always a hit with adults and kids. Serve with basmati rice/quinoa and steam/roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless check thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Directions

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker
  2. whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
