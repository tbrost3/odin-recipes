Bacon Wrapped Scallops

Description

Bacon wrapped scallops brings Surf 'N Turf as a bite-sized portion! This recipe is great during the summer/early fall when scallops are in season. It is a quick and easy way to get your homemade seafood fix with a lots of zest and a little zip! Let me know of any comments/suggestions you may have on the recipe.

Ingredients

  • sea scallops
  • hickory smoked bacon (cut in half)
  • toothpicks
  • stick of butter (melted)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 half lemon (squeezed)
  • 2 tsp parsley
  • 1 baking sheet
  • tinfoil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven on broil.
  2. Cut the hickory smoked bacon in half and wrap it around a fresh scallop. The bacon should go around the short side of the scallop and secured with a toothpick.
  3. Next, make the sauce by melting 1 stick of butter.
  4. Combine the minced garlic, salt, pepper, lemon juice, parsley, and red pepper flakes to the melted butter. Stir until well mixed.
  5. Individually submerge each bacon wrapped scallop into the sauce and place onto a baking sheet with tinfoil. If the sauce begins to separate, mix in between applications.
  6. Put the baking sheet in the oven for ~17 minutes or until the bacon is cooked through.
  7. Add any remaining sauce to the scallops after they are taken out of the oven.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
